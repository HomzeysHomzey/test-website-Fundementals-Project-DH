A deer or true deer is a hoofed ruminant ungulate of the family Cervidae. It is divided into subfamilies Cervinae which includes, among others, muntjac, elk , red deer, and fallow deer and Capreolinae which includes, among others reindeer , white-tailed deer, roe deer, and moose. Male deer of almost all species, as well as female reindeer, grow and shed new antlers each year. These antlers are bony extensions of the skull and are often used for combat between males.
